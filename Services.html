The Services We Offer

Inter-State Travel

Experience safe, reliable, and comfortable inter-state travel with GUO Transport, Nigeria’s trusted name in road transport. Whether you’re commuting for business, visiting family, or exploring new cities, our wide network of routes ensures you get to your destination with ease. We operate daily trips connecting major cities and towns across Nigeria, including Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Onitsha, Enugu, Owerri, Kano, and more. With modern vehicles, professional drivers, and a commitment to punctuality and passenger comfort, we make inter- state travel stress-free and enjoyable.

Charter Services

Need a vehicle for a special trip, event, or business purpose? With GUO Transport’s Charter Services, you enjoy the convenience, flexibility, and comfort of private travel — tailored to your schedule and destination. Whether it’s a corporate retreat, church outing, wedding, school excursion, or VIP movement, we provide reliable and affordable charter options for groups of any size.

NYSC Cash back

We appreciate the service and dedication of Nigeria’s youth corps members. That’s why we’ve introduced the NYSC Cash Back! When you traveling with GUO to the your NYSC camp, you earn a cash back on that trip.

Minor Booking

At GUO Transport Comfort and safety is everything, traveling with your child shouldn’t be financially burdensome, we provide discounted fares for your children when you travel with them.

We embrace creativity and continuous improvement, constantly seeking new and innovative approaches to optimize logistics processes, enhance efficiency, and adapt to evolving market dynamics.
